    Health Tips & Blogs

    Regular Exercise

    Ensure regular exercise for your pet's physical and mental health.

    Daily exercise helps prevent obesity, maintains joint health, and reduces behavioral problems. For dogs, aim for 30-60 minutes of activity. Cats benefit from 10-15 minutes of play sessions.

    Balanced Diet

    Maintain a nutritious and balanced diet for optimal health.

    A balanced diet should include proper portions of proteins, carbohydrates, and fats. Always provide fresh water and consult your vet for specific dietary needs.

    Regular Checkups

    Schedule annual veterinary check-ups for prevention.

    Annual check-ups help detect health issues early. Keep vaccination records updated and discuss any behavioral changes with your vet.
